🧠 Objective

Analyze user engagement behavior to improve retention, test completion, and revenue stability.

⚠️ Business Problem

  • Drop-off between Tests 7–10
  • Inconsistent engagement
  • Promotion-driven growth
  • Weak habit formation

📊 Analytical Approach (Tableau)

  • Retention and progression analysis
  • Engagement timing
  • Growth trends
💡 Core Insight: The problem is behavioral retention, not acquisition.

🚀 Recommendations

  • Extend free access
  • Introduce engagement rewards
  • Optimize upgrade timing
